MoodMender: Tailored Comfort for You.

Talk to a GPT-prompted psychologist on the internet for free!

MoodMender is a website that uses computer vision to track videos of your face, and uses a pretrained neural net to predict your emotion using this video.
ChatGPT was prompted to become a psychologist specialising in anxiety and depression. MoodMender allows you to talk using your voice to this customized gpt and listen to its responce (all using voice to text and text to voice). Furthermore the GPT is tailored to change its response based on the emotion the site belives you have!

The site was created to simulate talking to a real psychologist. Adding human elements such as allowing one to talk freely rather than having to type, and allowing gpt to respond to ones emotions automatically via changes in facial expression are done to make it feel more real, and convenient for the user.
